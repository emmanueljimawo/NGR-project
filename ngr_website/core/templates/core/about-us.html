About Us

Naija Grace Race is an innovative and goal oriented seasonal online and TV voting pageant show which gives opportunity and showcases positive attributes of talented Nigerian young men and women and award them with a cash price after completing a task and the task is base on entertainments such as dancing, singing, comedy etc. 


HERE IS HOW IT WORKS

A task will be given to the 6 contestant in which it will be showcase on a tv station and social medias for Nigerians to vote for within 24 hours, the person with the lowest vote will be evicted remaining 5 contestant, same goes to day 2 and day 3. The contest will last for 3 days after which 3 contestant will emerge as first, second and third Winners. Prices will be giving to the first, second and third with the highest Votes respectively
